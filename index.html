Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Your Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, software, and CRM automations.

What is Intelloworks and how is it different?

Intelloworks is a premium full-service digital agency and a CRM software suite. Instead of hiring a coding team, a marketing team, and subscribing to 10 separate pieces of software (CRMs, funnel builders, calendar widgets), we build your digital ecosystem and provide a consolidated CRM workspace that keeps all of your leads, automations, and calendars under one login.

Do you develop custom websites and e-commerce stores?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom high-performing website development, Shopify systems, WooCommerce, and tailored landing pages (similar to our work for La Cressida and Fomyo). All our web projects are fully integrated directly with your automation flows and sales pipelines.

How much money can I save by consolidating tools?

The average small business saves between $1,200 to $2,000 every month. By replacing separate accounts with ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, Calendly, ClickFunnels, Podium, and Zapier, you eliminate software redundancy, API failures, and separate licensing fees.

Do I get onboarding and configuration support?

Yes! We provide full, hands-on onboarding. We set up your pipelines, design your initial automation triggers (IntelloFlow), import your existing contacts, and ensure your team is trained to operate the dashboards effortlessly.
